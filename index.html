How to play 🗺️

🗺️
1. Pick a missionEach of the 11 missions is a topic led by a crew member — geography with Pep, space with Coco, animals with Delta, sports with Ozzy, logic with Vera, maths with Milo, science with Newton, language arts with Pia, art with Coral, design with Bodhi, and Spanish with Paco. Together they cover the real subjects of Grade 6.
❓
2. Answer 8 questionsYou have 20 seconds per question. Answer correctly to score points, and chain correct answers for streak bonuses.
🔀
3. Questions shuffle every timeEach mission has a bigger question bank than what you see in one round, so replaying pulls a fresh mix.
🎲
4. Try a Custom Mixed ChallengePick your own combination of topics from the mission map for a challenge round built just from what you choose.
👥
5. Play head-to-headSwitch to 2 Player mode and take turns on the exact same shuffled questions — whoever scores more wins the round.
🏅
6. Earn badges & a certificateScore well to earn stars in each mission. Collect all 11 badges (each player, in 2P mode) to unlock your Good Guy certificate.
💎
7. Collect gems & level upEvery correct answer earns gems, with bonuses for streaks, badges, and 3-star rounds. Gems build you up through 10 numbered levels — the higher your level, the tougher (and more rewarding) the questions get.
